Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI image to prompt tool.

General Questions

What is Image to Prompt Generator?

Image to Prompt Generator is a free AI tool that converts images into detailed, descriptive prompts that can be used with AI art generators like Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E, and Flux. Simply upload an image, and our AI will analyze it to create a comprehensive prompt.

How does the tool work?

Our tool uses advanced AI vision models (THUDM/GLM-4.1V-9B-Thinking) to analyze your uploaded image. The AI identifies visual elements like composition, style, lighting, colors, and subjects, then generates a detailed text prompt that describes these elements in a way that AI art generators can understand.

Is the tool really free?

Yes, completely free! There are no hidden costs, no premium tiers, and no usage limits. You can generate as many prompts as you need without any restrictions.

Technical Questions

What image formats are supported?

We support PNG, JPG, and WEBP formats. For best results, use high-quality images under 4MB in size. Higher resolution images generally produce more detailed prompts.

How accurate are the generated prompts?

Our AI model is specifically trained for prompt generation and achieves high accuracy in identifying visual elements. However, you may want to fine-tune the generated prompts based on your specific needs and the AI art generator you're using.

Which AI art platforms are supported?

Our prompts work with all major AI art generators including Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E, Flux, and others. The generated prompts are designed to be universally compatible across different platforms.

Do I need to create an account?

No account required! Simply visit the website, upload your image, and get your prompt instantly. No registration, login, or personal information needed.

Privacy & Security

Is my image data secure?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and security. Images are processed temporarily and immediately deleted after prompt generation. We don't store your images, personal data, or generated prompts on our servers.

Can I use the generated prompts commercially?

Yes, all generated prompts are yours to use freely for any purpose, including commercial projects, personal artwork, or client work. There are no usage restrictions or licensing fees.

Do you track my usage?

We only collect basic analytics to improve our service (like page views and general usage patterns). We don't track individual users or their specific image uploads.

Language Support

What languages are supported?

We support 11 languages: English, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Spanish, German, French, Portuguese, Arabic, Russian, Japanese, and Korean. You can select your preferred language before generating prompts.

Can I get prompts in multiple languages?

Currently, you can select one language per generation. If you need prompts in multiple languages, simply run the tool multiple times with different language selections.

Troubleshooting

The tool isn't working. What should I do?

Try refreshing the page and uploading your image again. Make sure your image is in a supported format (PNG, JPG, WEBP) and under 4MB. If the problem persists, contact us at support@imagetoprompt.app.

The generated prompt seems inaccurate. Why?

AI analysis can sometimes miss subtle details or interpret images differently. Try using higher quality images with clear subjects and good lighting. You can also manually edit the generated prompt to better match your vision.

Can I suggest improvements or report bugs?

Absolutely! We welcome feedback and suggestions. Please contact us at support@imagetoprompt.app with your suggestions or bug reports. Your input helps us improve the tool for everyone.
