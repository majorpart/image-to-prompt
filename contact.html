Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the Image to Prompt tool?

Simply upload an image or paste an image URL, select your preferred language, and click "Generate Prompt". Our AI will analyze your image and create a detailed prompt that you can use with AI art generators.

What image formats are supported?

We support PNG, JPG, and WEBP formats. Images should be under 4MB in size for optimal processing speed.

Is my image data secure?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy. Images are processed temporarily and immediately deleted after prompt generation. We don't store your images or personal data.

Can I use the generated prompts commercially?

Yes, all generated prompts are yours to use freely for any purpose, including commercial projects. There are no restrictions on usage.
